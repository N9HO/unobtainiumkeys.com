Operators building

Unobtainium Keys makes heirloom-grade CW Morse keys for the people who still reach for a paddle when the band opens.

Why we're here

We're ham radio operators and CW enthusiasts. We've also been on the receiving end of empty promises and underdelivered results — and we've watched too many operators spend hard-earned money on keys and customer service that didn't come close to representing the equity being exchanged.

Unobtainium Keys is our answer to that. Three builders who couldn't find the key — or the treatment — we wanted, so we set out to make both: keys built without compromise and in small numbers, the way the great keys have always been made, backed by people who actually answer.

A key is the one piece of the station you touch every second you're on the air. It should feel like an extension of your hand and outlast the rig it sits beside. That's the bar we build to, and the reason we back every key for life.
