What we warrant

Every Unobtainium key is designed, machined, finished, and inspected by hand. We warrant each key to be free from defects in materials and workmanship for as long as Unobtainium Keys remains in operation as a company.

Transferable

This warranty covers the key, not just its first owner. If your key changes hands, the warranty goes with it.

What we’ll do

If a defect in our materials or workmanship appears, we will repair or replace the key at our discretion, at no charge to you. When a claim is deemed a genuine warranty issue, we cover shipping both ways.

Wear and maintenance

Any component that moves or flexes in normal operation — including pivots, contacts, springs, paddle arms, and adjustment hardware — is considered a wear item. We warrant wear items against defects in materials and manufacture, but not against wear, fatigue, or loss of adjustment from use. Replacement wear parts and routine service are always available from us.

What voids the warranty

Modifying a key voids its warranty permanently, and the warranty does not transfer to any key that has been modified. Normal disassembly for cleaning, adjustment, or replacing our wear parts is not a modification — modification means altering, drilling, re-machining, refinishing, or substituting non-Unobtainium parts.

The warranty also does not cover damage from drops, misuse, neglect, corrosion, or service performed outside our shop.

We can still help

Even if a key has been modified, neglected, or damaged, we offer repair services to bring it back to life. Contact us for an estimate.

Making a claim

Contact us before sending anything back so we can sort out whether it’s a warranty repair or a service repair. For valid warranty claims, we pay inbound and outbound shipping. For non-warranty repairs, the customer covers shipping; we’ll quote any parts and labor before starting work.
